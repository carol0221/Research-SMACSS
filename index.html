About EXO

Exo is South Korean boy group formed by SM Entertainment They debuted in 2012 with twelve members, separated into two sub-groups: EXO-K and EXO-M, performing in Korean and Mandarin respectively. The current line-up consists of nine members: Xiumin, Suho, Lay, Chen, D.O, Kai, Baekhyun, Chanyeol, and Sehun
